# SpaceRun

**Survive the infinite void. How far can you fly?**

SpaceRun is a fast-paced, endless space runner that throws you straight into the heart of the cosmos — no downloads, no installs, no fuss. Just pure, addictive arcade action that runs right in your browser, on your phone, on your tablet, or on your desktop.

Hold to climb. Release to dive. That's all it takes. But don't let the simplicity fool you: as your distance grows, so does the speed — and the universe gets meaner. Jagged asteroid fields drift toward you, the tunnel walls twist and tighten, and only the sharpest pilots make it past the next horizon.

## Why you'll love it

- 🚀 **Pick up and play** — one button (or one thumb). Perfect for a 30-second break or a 30-minute grind.
- 🌌 **Endless, procedural galaxies** — every run is a brand-new stretch of space, generated on the fly. No two flights are ever the same.
- 🛰️ **Collect & master a fleet** — start with the trusty *Scout* and unlock nine more ships, from the nimble *Falcon* to the legendary *Singularity*. The further you fly, the fancier your hangar gets.
- 💫 **Three parallax layers** — drifting stars, glowing nebulae, and a living local space that makes every jump feel cinematic.
- 🌍 **Speaks your language** — available in English, Portuguese, and Spanish, with automatic detection.
- ☕ **Made with love** — and if you enjoy the ride, you can fuel the next update with a coffee on Ko-Fi or Buy Me a Coffee.

## How to play

- **Desktop:** hold the **Spacebar** to thrust upward, release to drop.
- **Mobile / Tablet:** **touch and hold** the screen to rise, lift your finger to fall.
- Dodge the asteroids. Stay clear of the closing walls. Go the distance.

The deeper you go, the faster it gets — so breathe, time your taps, and chase that high score.

## Ready to run?

Jump in, pick your ship, and see how many meters of open space you can conquer. The void is waiting. 🌠
